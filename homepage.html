WELCOME TO PERSATUAN SENI SILAT GAYONG MALAYSIA (PSSGM), IIUM

Persatuan Seni Silat Gayong Malaysia (PSSGM) was one of the co-curriculum activities for IIUM students. This club has been established since 1995. This association is under the umbrella of the National Malaysian Gayong Silat Association established by Dato' Meor Abdul Rahman bin Uda Mohd Hashim. Silat Seni Gayong has various learning breakdowns such as movement basics, avoidance basics, jump basics and deadly attacks. Silat Seni Gayong offers self-defense knowledge that also teaches its practitioners leadership, discipline and courage.
